About Cooking FilAm

Cooking FilAm is here to help you rediscover your roots or explore a new culture through food. As a Caregiver brand, we aim to guide and support you every step of the way, whether you're a beginner or an experienced cook.

Our mission is to preserve traditional Filipino recipes while making them accessible to modern home cooks. With step-by-step guidance, AI-powered tools, and a supportive community, we make cooking Filipino-American dishes easy and enjoyable.

Featured Recipes

Explore our collection of authentic and modern Filipino-American recipes. From grandma’s traditional dishes to quick and easy meals, we have something for everyone.

Featured Recipes

  • Chicken Adobo: A savory and tangy Filipino dish made with chicken marinated in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices.
  • Arroz Caldo: A comforting rice porridge infused with ginger, garlic, and chicken, perfect for cold days.
  • Lumpia (Spring Rolls): Crispy and flavorful Filipino spring rolls filled with meat and vegetables.
  • Pancit (Noodles): Stir-fried noodles with meat and vegetables, symbolizing long life and prosperity.
